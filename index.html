Welcome to Haukai Restaurant

Welcome to Haukai Restaurant, where the rich flavors of Māori kai blend seamlessly with the stunning backdrop of the Bay of Islands.

Founded by a passionate couple dedicated to crafting a culinary experience that resonates with both locals and visitors alike, Haukai is a labor of love that goes beyond mere dining.

Operating exclusively in the evenings from Wednesday to Sunday, our restaurant boasts a diverse menu that seamlessly weaves together traditional Māori and Pākeha kai options, accompanied by a curated selection of beverages.

Explore the essence of Te Ao Māori as we infuse cultural elements, such as karakia for blessing food, into every aspect of our service.

Digital Immersion in Māori Culinary Experience

As we embark on this gastronomic journey, Haukai is not just a physical space—it's an interactive experience waiting to unfold on our new web application.

Discover the allure of Māori culture at your fingertips, immersing yourself in a digital realm that mirrors the warmth and authenticity of our physical establishment.

By embracing technology, we aim to enhance your connection with us. Say goodbye to traditional phone bookings as we introduce a seamless online submission system, saving both time and resources.

Join us in reducing our ecological footprint while enjoying the convenience of reserving your spot with just a click.
