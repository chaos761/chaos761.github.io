Meet the Visionaries

Nestled in the vibrant Bay of Islands, New Zealand, Amara Kerehoma and Basil Kerehoma, an adventurous and flavor-driven couple, embarked on a culinary odyssey that would captivate the palates of both locals and travelers.

Their love story unfolded amidst the rhythmic waves, where Amara and Basil explored the abundant marine life and forged a profound connection with the sea. Influenced by the rich traditions of the Māori, the indigenous people of New Zealand, they aspired to create a dining experience that embodied the essence of their shared passion.

Amara and Basil felt a calling to bring these flavors to life, paying homage to the Māori spirits that guided their journey. With a sense of destiny, they opened the doors to "Haukai," their seaside haven, where each dish was a masterpiece crafted with love. The menu, infused with ingredients echoing the whispers of Māori traditions, became a testament to their divine connection and the extraordinary journey that led them to share the enchantment of the Bay of Islands with the world.
